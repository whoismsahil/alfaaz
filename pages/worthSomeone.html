Worth someone


My heart breaks,
When I lay in bed at nights
and wonders why I am not enough
or not worthy for that only one
person whom I give my whole
world to.
That ruins me.

Do you how much pain
it takes to hold that 'I Love You'
on my tongue each and every time
when we talk just cause
I know that you are not going
to reply me back.
Even if I beg you for the mercy
or even if I'll be on my
death bed.

I burn my eyes on the screens
so that I can become something
which can make you think that
I am also worth someone to
love too.
Every moment I try to
become a better person
for you and for the coming
future.

It makes me cry during
sleepless nights where
I only think that what is
still missing to show my
inner feelings.
I feel the urges to hold your
hands and to hold you
closer to me so that
no one can ever rob you this
time from me.

People say that it's the
waiting time which tells you
about the amount of love
I have for you, guess what!
I've waited and I am still waiting
till this very next moment.
5 years, 1 month, 10 days,
19 hours, 20 minutes and
the remaining seconds.
Till you'll read this many more
moments would have
been passed.

Every single time my screen
brightens up with some notification
I tend to hope that it's your message
saying that I am the one who
finally deserves your love, or
even just a message sharing
about your day.
I might be the fastest person
to reply back to your messages
But darling it's just the love
towards you which makes
me to.

You might be thinking
that I may be bluffing
or lying to the top most
level but you know that
I am not, when you were
not there then I literally went
on the worst track.
The level of love you can
think about that you are
the only girl my mother is
aware of.

I am not a person who'll
try to prove his love to you
and I am definitely not a
person who like extra
materialism.
I just want to be loved
by you, just want to feel
special that there is you
who loves me the way
I Love You.
