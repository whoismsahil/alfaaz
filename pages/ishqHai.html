Koi dil ko bha jaaye wo,
Ishq hai.
Aur jo sab kuch bhula de wo bhi,
Ishq hai.

Wo sadko pe intezaar krna,
Ishq hai.
Aur wo saamne aa jaane par chup jaana bhi,
Ishq hai.

Wo muskurana,
Ishq hai.
Aur wo aankho ka milna bhi,
Ishq hai.

Lafz bayaan na kr paana,
Ishq hai.
Aur nazron se hi bol dena bhi,
Ishq hai.

Wo roz ki mulaqaate,
Ishq hai.
Aur wo justajoo mein tadapna bhi,
Ishq hai.

Uski qurbat me sab bhool jaana,
Ishq hai.
Aur uske choo lena par kho jaana bhi,
Ishq hai.

Jo inaayat lage wo,
Ishq hai.
Aur jo seher ka noor lage wo bhi,
Ishq hai.
