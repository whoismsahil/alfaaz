Din ki shruwaat


Mere din ki shruwaat bhi teri awaaz se hoti hai aur mere din ka annt bhi...

Maanta hu ki main world ka best nhi,
Par han, koshish jaroor krunga ki main tere liye best banun.

Maanta hu ki meri utni hassiyat nhi,
Par han, itna jaroor hai ki teri harr khwaish ko poori krne ki koshish krunga taki tujhe koi kami na ho.

Maanta hu ki main wo 'Everything Remember' wala banda nhi,
Par han, koshish jaroor krunga ki tere saath bitaye sabhi palon ko main yaad rakkhon.

Maanta hu ki main zyada bolta nhi, har waqt tmhe bolta hun ki 'Tum Bolo - Tum Bolo',
Par han, itna jaroor hai ki tere paas hone par mere alfaaz rukte nhi... Bs mann krta hai ki je bhar ke hass lun aur roo lun.

Maanta hu ki mujhe surprises dene nhi aata,
Par han, itna jaroor hai ki mere achanak se saamne aa jaane se jb tm khush ho jaati ho to... Maano... Maano ye duniya khushi mana rhi ho.

Maanta hu ki main koi accha wala joker nhi,
Par han, itna jaroor hai ki jab tum mere unn bedhange se baaton pr jee khol kr hass deti ho to... Jaise... lagta hai maano poori zindagi ka maqsad hi poora ho gya ho.

Maanta hun ki mujhe nhi pta ki kya hoga aage, aur na hi main jaan na caahta hu,
Par han, itna jaroor hai ki tu uss samandar me ek kashti ki tarah hai jo iss sahil pr aakr hi rukegi.

Kyunki...
Mere din ki shruwaat bhi teri awaaz se hoti hai aur mere din ka annt bhi...
